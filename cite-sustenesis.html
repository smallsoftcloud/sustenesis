Concept attribution

Sustenesis is an original philosophical term created by Geoffrey Chen. Sustenesis Theory is the philosophical framework developed around this term. The Chinese counterpart of Sustenesis is 维成, and the Chinese name of Sustenesis Theory is 维成论.
