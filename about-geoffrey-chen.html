About Geoffrey Chen

Creator of Sustenesis and Sustenesis Theory.

Geoffrey Chen is an independent researcher working at the intersection of epistemology, ontology, systems design, and artificial intelligence, with a background in software engineering. He is the creator of Sustenesis and Sustenesis Theory.

His work examines how knowledge, truth, meaning, value, consciousness, and intelligent systems can be understood not as isolated possessions of a knowing subject, but as stable coherences sustained under conditions of constraint, correction, and effective operation.

In Sustenesis Theory, he develops a philosophical framework for understanding existence, knowledge, consciousness, value, and artificial intelligence through the formation and maintenance of coherent structures. The theory extends beyond the traditional subject-centred model of knowledge while preserving its mature forms.

Related projects

Sustenesis Theory is closely connected with Geoffrey Chen's broader philosophical project concerning knowledge after the weakening of the traditional knowing subject. The theory provides a deeper structural foundation for thinking about non-human knowledge systems, artificial intelligence, and the conditions under which coherence can exist without always depending on a human knower.
